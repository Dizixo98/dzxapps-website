Privacy Policy

Last updated: 01.07.2026

DZX Apps ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information MainzIQ stores, how it is used, how it is protected, and how you can control or delete it.

Information We Store

MainzIQ stores information you enter into the app, including vehicle data, service records, mileage, notes and app settings.

If you use the app without an account, your data is stored only locally on your device.

If you sign in with Google, your data is securely stored in Google Firebase Cloud Firestore to provide backup and synchronization across your devices.

MainzIQ may also retrieve vehicle information from the Norwegian Public Roads Administration (Statens vegvesen) when you choose to look up a vehicle by its registration number. Retrieved information may include vehicle specifications, fuel type, VIN, first registration date and next mandatory inspection (EU inspection). This information is used solely to populate and update your vehicle records.

Analytics

MainzIQ uses Google Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage information, such as app launches, screen views and feature usage. This information helps us improve the app's quality, stability and overall user experience.

Firebase Analytics is not used for advertising, personalized marketing or user profiling. The content you store in MainzIQ, including your vehicles, service history, mileage, notes and photos, is never analyzed for analytics purposes.

MainzIQ does not currently use Firebase Crashlytics or any other crash-reporting service.

How We Use Data

The information is used solely to provide the app's functionality, including vehicle management, maintenance history, mileage tracking, statistics, data export, cloud synchronization and automatic updates of vehicle information retrieved from Statens vegvesen when enabled by the user.

MainzIQ also uses Google Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage information, such as app launches and feature usage, in order to improve the app's quality, stability and overall user experience.

Vehicle registration numbers entered by the user are transmitted securely to the Norwegian Public Roads Administration (Statens vegvesen) only when the user explicitly requests vehicle lookup or has enabled automatic vehicle data updates.

Data Security

Data is stored either locally on your device or in Firebase Cloud Firestore when synchronization is enabled. Communication with our cloud services is encrypted in transit, and we use security measures provided by Google Firebase to help protect stored data. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission can guarantee absolute security.

Cloud Storage and Synchronization

Automatic updates from Statens vegvesen only update vehicle information already stored in MainzIQ and do not create additional cloud data beyond your existing vehicle records.

Important:
Images attached to vehicles or service records may be stored locally on your device and may not be included in cloud backups or synchronization. If you uninstall the app or lose your device, locally stored images cannot be recovered.

Your Rights and Data Deletion

You can manage or delete your data at any time:

  • Delete individual vehicles or service records directly within the app — this permanently removes them from your device and, if signed in, from the cloud.
  • Sign out of your Google account in the app to stop future cloud synchronization.
  • Uninstall the app to remove all locally stored data from your device.
  • Request full deletion of your cloud-stored account data by contacting us at the email below — we will process such requests within 30 days.

Children's Privacy

MainzIQ is not directed at children and has no specific age restriction for general use. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, please contact us so we can delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the app evolves. Material changes will be announced within the app. Continued use of MainzIQ after such changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have any questions regarding privacy, data processing or would like to request deletion of your account and associated cloud data, please contact DZX Apps at Support@DZXapps.com.
