Privacy Policy

Last updated: 22.06.2026

DZX Apps ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information MainzIQ stores, how it is used, and how it is protected.

Information We Store

MainzIQ stores information that you enter into the app, including vehicle data, service records, mileage and app settings. If you use the app without an account, data is stored only locally on your device. If you sign in with Google, your data may also be securely stored in the cloud for backup and synchronization across devices.

How We Use Data

The information is used solely to provide app functionality, including vehicle management, maintenance history, statistics, data export and synchronization across devices.

Data Sharing

MainzIQ does not sell, rent or share your personal data with third parties. When using Google Sign-In, Google Firebase is used for authentication and storage of synchronized data.

Data Security

Data is stored either locally on your device or in Firebase Cloud Firestore when synchronization is enabled. We use security measures provided by Google Firebase to help protect stored data. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission can guarantee absolute security.

Cloud Storage and Synchronization

When you sign in with Google, vehicle data, service history, mileage and other recorded information may be securely synchronized and stored in the cloud. This allows your data to be restored when changing devices or reinstalling the app. Images may be stored locally on the device and are not necessarily included in cloud synchronization.

Important:
Images attached to vehicles or service records may be stored locally on your device and may not be included in cloud backups or synchronization.

Contact

If you have any questions regarding privacy or data processing in MainzIQ, please contact DZX Apps at support@dzxapps.com.
