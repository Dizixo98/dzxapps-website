Privacy Policy

Last updated: 22.06.2026

DZX Apps ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information MainzIQ stores, how it is used, how it is protected, and how you can control or delete it.

Information We Store

MainzIQ stores information that you enter into the app, including vehicle data, service records, mileage, photos and app settings. If you use the app without an account, data is stored only locally on your device. If you sign in with Google, your data may also be securely stored in the cloud for backup and synchronization across devices.

We do not request or collect your name, contact list, location, or any information beyond what is needed for the Google Sign-In process itself (your Google account identifier and email address, used solely to link your synced data to your account).

No Analytics or Crash Reporting

MainzIQ does not use Firebase Analytics, Google Analytics, Firebase Crashlytics, or any other third-party analytics or crash-reporting tool. We do not automatically collect usage statistics, device telemetry, or behavioral data. The only data processed by the app is the data you explicitly enter.

How We Use Data

The information is used solely to provide app functionality, including vehicle management, maintenance history, statistics, data export and synchronization across devices.

Data Sharing

MainzIQ does not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes. When using Google Sign-In, Google Firebase is used strictly as our infrastructure provider for authentication and storage of synchronized data, under Google's own data processing terms.

Data Security

Data is stored either locally on your device or in Firebase Cloud Firestore when synchronization is enabled. We use security measures provided by Google Firebase, including encryption in transit, to help protect stored data. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission can guarantee absolute security.

Cloud Storage and Synchronization

When you sign in with Google, vehicle data, service history, mileage and other recorded information may be securely synchronized and stored in the cloud. This allows your data to be restored when changing devices or reinstalling the app. Images may be stored locally on the device and are not necessarily included in cloud synchronization.

Important:
Images attached to vehicles or service records may be stored locally on your device and may not be included in cloud backups or synchronization. If you uninstall the app or lose your device, locally stored images cannot be recovered.

Your Rights and Data Deletion

You can manage or delete your data at any time:

  • Delete individual vehicles or service records directly within the app — this permanently removes them from your device and, if signed in, from the cloud.
  • Sign out of your Google account in the app to stop future cloud synchronization.
  • Uninstall the app to remove all locally stored data from your device.
  • Request full deletion of your cloud-stored account data by contacting us at the email below — we will process such requests within 30 days.

Children's Privacy

MainzIQ is not directed at children and has no specific age restriction for general use. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, please contact us so we can delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the app evolves. Material changes will be announced within the app. Continued use of MainzIQ after such changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have any questions regarding privacy or data processing in MainzIQ, please contact DZX Apps at support@DZXapps.com.
